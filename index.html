Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling roguelike adventure game!

About the Game

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face increasingly challenging waves of monsters. Level up, collect powerful abilities, and see how long you can survive!

Key Features

  • Endless survival gameplay
  • Multiple character abilities
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Unique power-ups and combinations

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience orbs to level up. Choose new abilities as you progress and create powerful combinations to survive longer.

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid enemy attacks
  • Collect experience orbs to level up quickly
  • Choose complementary abilities
  • Watch out for elite monsters
