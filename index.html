Monster Survivors

Embark on an epic survival adventure in this thrilling roguelike game!

About the Game

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face increasingly challenging waves of monsters. Level up, collect powerful abilities, and see how long you can survive!

Key Features

  • Endless survival gameplay with increasing difficulty
  • Diverse character abilities and playstyles
  • Dynamic progression system
  • Strategic power-up combinations

How to Play

Master your survival skills using WASD or arrow keys to navigate. Engage in automatic combat against nearby enemies while collecting experience orbs to level up. Strategically select new abilities as you progress and create powerful combinations to extend your survival time.

Pro Tips

  • Stay mobile to dodge enemy attacks
  • Prioritize collecting experience orbs for faster leveling
  • Build synergistic ability combinations
  • Be cautious of elite monsters and their special abilities
